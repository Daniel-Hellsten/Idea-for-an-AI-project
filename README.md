# my_projects
Building AI course project

Final project for the Building AI course

### **Summary**

Chico Swap is an assistant that learns a user’s preferences and habits to make everyday life smoother. It acts as an intelligent bridge by swapping anonymized user data with local service providers to create real-time, personalized offers.

### **Background**

Problem: Consumers lack the time to find services that perfectly match their tastes, especially during short breaks.

Large chains are often rigid and cannot adapt to individual needs or change their offers as quickly as small businesses.

Speed as an Advantage: 
Small companies can react instantly to a local lead, whereas large organizations have slower decision-making processes.
City centers are full of small shops that are physically closer to the user than big suburban malls.

Importance: 
This creates a fair environment where speed and specialization allow small businesses to win.

### **How is it used**

Imagine a situation where a user on a lunch break needs a specific service (like a restaurant or a hairdresser) nearby.
Chico Swap shares anonymized preferences with local businesses within a short radius.

-> Businesses must react fast with a price and a service that matches the user’s profile.
-> The user gets a list of the best-rated and most affordable options that fit their schedule perfectly.


### **Data and AI techniques**

The system depends on the user's calendar and deep personal preferences (e.g., exact food tastes or style choices).

The AI continuously learns from the user’s choices and feedback to become a better reflection of their needs.

-> Chico Swap sends anonymous data to businesses "along the user’s path" and requires them to provide rapid, matching offers.


### **Challenges**

Because the system requires a "fast offer," it is difficult for any single player to capture the entire market.

-> Large chains may struggle to compete in a system that values immediate, localized flexibility over mass marketing.
-> Ensuring that the rapid offers from small businesses consistently meet the high quality expected by the user.

### **What next**

As the AI evolves, it becomes more accurate in predicting what the user wants before they even ask.

Market Balance: 
The goal is to create a sustainable ecosystem where quality and speed are the primary factors for success.

### **Acknowledgments**

The idea is based on empowering the individual consumer while giving local small businesses a technological edge in a fast-paced world.
